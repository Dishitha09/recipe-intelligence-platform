Coastal Coconut Rice Cookbook Page
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked rice
1 cup coconut
1 tbsp oil
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tbsp chana dal
2 green chillies
1 pinch salt
Instructions:
Cool the cooked rice so the grains stay separate.
Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
Add chana dal and saute until it turns golden.
Add green chillies and grated coconut, then cook briefly.
Add rice and salt, then mix without breaking the grains.
Cover for two minutes on low heat.
Serve coconut rice warm with pickle or curd.
