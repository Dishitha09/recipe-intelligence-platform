Karnataka Lemon Rice Audio Transcript
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked rice
2 tbsp oil
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tbsp peanuts
2 green chillies
0.5 tsp turmeric
2 tbsp lemon juice
0.5 tsp salt
Instructions:
Spread the cooked rice on a plate and let it cool completely.
Heat oil in a pan and splutter the mustard seeds.
Add peanuts and fry until they turn golden.
Add green chillies and turmeric, then stir for a few seconds.
Switch off the heat, add rice and salt, and mix gently.
Pour lemon juice over the rice and toss until evenly coated.
Rest for five minutes before serving.
