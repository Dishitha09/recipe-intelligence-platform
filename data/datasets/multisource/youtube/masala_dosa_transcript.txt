Masala Dosa YouTube Transcript
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
0.25 cup urad dal
0.25 tsp fenugreek seeds
0.5 tsp salt
2 tbsp oil
Instructions:
Rinse the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds until the water runs clear.
Soak the rice and dal separately for 4 to 6 hours.
Grind them to a smooth batter with just enough water.
Mix the batter with salt and ferment overnight until light and bubbly.
Heat a dosa tawa, spread a ladle of batter thinly, drizzle oil, and cook until crisp.
Fold the dosa and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
