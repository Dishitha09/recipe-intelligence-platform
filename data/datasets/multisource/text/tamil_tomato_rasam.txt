Tamil Tomato Rasam

Ingredients:
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 tsp tamarind paste
1 tsp rasam powder
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
8 curry leaves
2 cups water
1 tsp ghee
Salt to taste

Instructions:
Crush cumin seeds and black pepper coarsely.
Boil tomatoes, tamarind paste, turmeric powder, rasam powder, salt, and water until the tomatoes soften.
Mash the tomatoes lightly and simmer until the rasam smells aromatic.
Heat ghee in a small pan and add curry leaves.
Pour the tempering into the rasam and serve hot with rice.
