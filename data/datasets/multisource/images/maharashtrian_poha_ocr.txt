Maharashtrian Kanda Poha Recipe Card
Ingredients:
2 cups poha
1 onion
2 tbsp peanuts
1 tsp mustard seeds
0.5 tsp turmeric
1 tbsp oil
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 pinch salt
Instructions:
Rinse poha quickly and drain it in a colander.
Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
Add peanuts and fry until lightly crisp.
Add chopped onion and cook until translucent.
Add turmeric and drained poha, then mix gently.
Cover and steam for two minutes on low heat.
Finish with lemon juice and serve warm.
